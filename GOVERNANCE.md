# Governance (ITM AIR Lab)

This document defines roles, decision-making, and committee structures.

Phases
- Initial (0–6 months): Student-run, with a Management Committee (college HODs) supporting approvals and resources.
- Formal (after 6 months): Control Committee formed to guide long-term direction.

Management Committee (temporary)
- Composition: college HODs or designated admins.
- Role: administrative approvals, access to institutional resources, non-intrusive oversight.

Control Committee (permanent)
- Composition:
  - 1–2 Senior faculty members
  - External AI researchers (advisors)
  - Industry advisors (as available)
  - Permanent student research fellows
  - Lab Director (chair)
- Responsibilities:
  - Long-term strategy & ethics
  - Funding and partnerships
  - Final approvals for major projects and compute allocations

Student roles
- Department Leads (Research, Data, Development)
- Contributors
- Project owners (for individual research projects)

Decision model
- Routine research & experiments: department lead approval.
- Major resource allocation, partnerships, or ethical risk: Control Committee signoff.

Elections & term lengths
- Department leads: elected/appointed every 6 months.
- Student fellows: reviewed annually.
